FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which area does Veloce Mobil cover?

We serve the whole of Switzerland. A travel surcharge may apply outside our core area.

Do I need to provide water or power on-site?

Currently, yes: we need a water connection (e.g. a garden hose) and a power outlet from you. You hand us the key briefly, we clean your vehicle inside and out, and then return it to you – you can check the result right away.

How long does a detailing session take?

Depending on the package and vehicle size, between 30 minutes (Basic) and 3.5 hours (Premium, large vehicles).

How can I pay?

Conveniently on-site by card or Twint, or online in advance when booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, free of charge up to 24 hours before the agreed appointment.

Is my vehicle insured during the detailing?

Yes, our work is covered under our business liability insurance.
